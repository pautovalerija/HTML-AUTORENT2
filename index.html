CAR RENTAL

To rent a car from us, you need to call the phone number +37255555555 and tell what car you need and for what period. After clarifying all the details, we will confirm the booking, and a car will be assigned to you for the specified period. You can also book a car yourself on our website using a convenient booking form. To do this, select the desired rental date(s), then select the one you like from the list of cars available for that period and fill in the fields with the detailing data. In this case, a booking confirmation will be informed by e-mail or by phone. When receiving a car, you will need to provide a driver’s license and an ID. Once all the documents are completed, you will be asked to pay for the car rental, after which you will receive keys to the car. The process of receiving a car does not take more than 10 minutes.
